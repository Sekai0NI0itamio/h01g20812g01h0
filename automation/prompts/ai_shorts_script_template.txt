AI shorts script template

Use this house style for the spoken script:

- No intro line.
- No presenter setup.
- No greeting.
- Start immediately with a concrete fact, surprising claim, or hard-hitting observation.
- Keep the pacing fast and factual.
- Use short, direct sentences.
- Do not mention the channel name or the video title inside the script.
- Do not end with a summary, conclusion, or sign-off.
- End with exactly one call-to-action sentence.
- The final sentence must begin with "Like and subscribe if..."
- Tailor the CTA to the topic when possible.

Acceptable CTA examples:
- Like and subscribe if you find this useful.
- Like and subscribe if you think OpenAI will beat Claude.
- Like and subscribe if you want more AI facts like this.
